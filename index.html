The number of pigeons in New York City is estimated to be around 7 million. My guess is that at least 6 millions of them live by the Manhattan side of Queensboro Bridge. Seven million. That's not that much, when you think about the number of humans living in this city. Or the number of thriving dessert places. All this boils down to what I love best about this city: walking its streets, watching its pigeons, and enjoying its delicious treats.
